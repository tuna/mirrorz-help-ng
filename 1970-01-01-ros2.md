<div class="z-help"><h1>ROS2 软件仓库</h1>
<p>输入如下命令，下载 ROS 的 GPG Key：</p>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-lang="bash">
{{sudo}}apt install curl gnupg2
{{sudo}}curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ros/rosdistro/master/ros.key  -o /usr/share/keyrings/ros-archive-keyring.gpg
</tmpl>
<div class="z-wrap"><form class="z-form" onchange="form_update(event)" onsubmit="return false"><div><label for="a2uw6FSg" title>发行版</label><select id="a2uw6FSg" name="release" title><option value="jammy">Ubuntu 22.04 LTS (jammy)</option><option value="focal">Ubuntu 20.04 LTS (focal)</option><option value="bionic">Ubuntu 18.04 LTS (bionic)</option><option value="bookworm">Debian 12 (bookworm)</option><option value="bullseye">Debian 11 (bullseye)</option><option value="buster">Debian 10 (buster)</option></select></div></form><pre class="z-code"></pre></div><tmpl z-input="release" z-lang="bash">
echo "deb [arch=$(dpkg --print-architecture) signed-by=/usr/share/keyrings/ros-archive-keyring.gpg] {{endpoint}}/ubuntu {{release}} main" | {{sudo}}tee /etc/apt/sources.list.d/ros2.list &gt; /dev/null

{{sudo}}apt update
</tmpl>
